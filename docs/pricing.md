# Pricing

The most important thing on this page: **self-hosting Formsmith is free, forever, and it is
not a downgrade.** The open-source core is the full product — you are never paying to unlock
a feature that already exists in the box. See [Why open source](why-open-source.md) for the
commitment behind that.

## Self-host — free, forever

Run the whole thing yourself with `docker compose up`.

- The complete builder, AI interviewer, respondent runtime, theming, logic, and scoring.
- Webhooks, REST API + OpenAPI, CSV/JSON export.
- **No** response limits, seat limits, or feature flags gated behind a license key.
- Your data stays on your infrastructure — see [Your data](your-data.md).

Cost: your own server. That's the deal.

## Hosted — *coming soon*

A managed tier for people who'd rather not run a container: backups, updates, and operations
handled for you. It's the same Formsmith, hosted by us — and paying for it funds continued
development of the open core.

It isn't live yet. **[Join the waitlist](https://formsmith.app)** and we'll let you know when
it opens. Until then, self-hosting gives you the full product today.

## Enterprise — commercial modules

For organizations with compliance and scale requirements, commercially-licensed
[enterprise modules](../modules) add SSO/SAML, SCIM, RBAC, audit logs, white-labeling, and
data-residency controls on top of the open core. These are add-ons for larger teams — not
gates on anything you can self-host.

Interested? Reach out: **gnana097@gmail.com**.

---

> **Why isn't self-host just listed as the "free tier" in a pricing table?** Because it isn't
> a tier of a paid product — it's the product. Framing it as the cheapest column would imply
> the paid options are "more Formsmith," and they aren't. Hosted sells convenience;
> enterprise sells organizational features. The core is the same, and it's open.
